Locations of Interest

Q: Where is the wedding going to be?

A: In Peabody, Massachusetts! It's a town about 30 minutes north of Boston, and only a few minutes away from nearby Salem, a very popular Fall destination.

Q: How can I get to Peabody?

To get to Massachusetts, you can fly into Logan airport (BOS). From there, take route 1 north, or take 93 north until it hits 95, then go east.

Q: What can I do in Peabody?

A lot! There's a whole downtown street, with a library, a several restaurants.

There's also a lot to do in nearby Salem! Witch tours, cute shops, and plenty of food!

You are also a quick train ride to downtown Boston!
